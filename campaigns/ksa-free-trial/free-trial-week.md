## SECTION 5: FAQ (4 Cards)

### Q1: "What if my child cries or won't leave my side?"
That's exactly why we give you a full week. Day one is about getting comfortable. Day two, most kids walk in with confidence. By day three, they don't want to leave. Our coaches work with first-timers every single week — there's no rush and no judgment.

### Q2: "What does my child need to bring?"
Just comfortable clothes and sneakers. No cleats needed — our indoor turf is designed for regular shoes. We provide all the soccer equipment. Same gear for every class during your trial week.

### Q3: "Is this really free? What's the catch?"
No catch. Up to 3 classes, zero cost, no payment information, no commitment. We offer a full trial week because one class isn't always enough to see the real magic. Most families know by class two.

### Q4: "Not sure which program to pick?"
No problem. Tell us your child's age when you book and we'll place them in the right group. Every program is age-right — designed for exactly where your child is right now. Your child stays in the same group for all trial classes.
