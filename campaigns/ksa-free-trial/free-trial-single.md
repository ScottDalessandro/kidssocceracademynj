## SECTION 5: FAQ (4 Cards)

### Q1: "What if my child cries or won't leave my side?"
It happens — and our coaches are ready for it. They work with first-timers every single week. Some kids jump right in. Others need a few minutes. Either way, there's no rush and no judgment. Most kids are smiling before the session ends.

### Q2: "What does my child need to bring?"
Just comfortable clothes and sneakers. No cleats needed — our indoor turf is designed for regular shoes. We provide all the soccer equipment.

### Q3: "Is this really free? What's the catch?"
No catch. No payment information. No commitment. We offer a free trial because we're confident in what happens on the turf. One class is usually all it takes.

### Q4: "Not sure which program to pick?"
No problem. Tell us your child's age when you book and we'll place them in the right group. Every program is age-right — designed for exactly where your child is right now.
