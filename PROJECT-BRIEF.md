## Site Purpose

The website exists to accomplish one primary objective: **convert searching parents into trial bookings and then into paying members.**

Parent journey: Search "kids soccer near me" → find site → evaluate safety/professionalism → book free trial → attend → enroll.

Every page should reduce friction and build confidence along this path.

## Current Site Weaknesses (Fix)

### Navigation (Critical)
- 13 nav items — every program listed individually
- Inconsistent navigation across pages (homepage nav differs from schedule page nav)
- Mobile hamburger menu is an endless scroll
- **Fix:** 7-8 max top-level items with dropdown for programs: Home | Programs (dropdown) | Schedule | About Us | Free Trial | Birthday Parties | Contact

### Trust Signals (Critical — biggest gap)
- **33 five-star Google reviews exist but are INVISIBLE on the website** — this is the #1 pitch point
- Zero coach/instructor bios anywhere — no names, photos, credentials, licenses
- No safety info — no background checks, insurance, CPR/first aid, concussion protocols mentioned
- Two vague unattributed testimonials on Free Trial page
- Unsourced "Voted #1 Toddler & Youth Soccer Program" claim — undermines credibility
- **Fix:** Embed live Google reviews, add coach bios page, add safety/credentials section

### CTAs & Conversion Path (Critical)
- "Book a Free Trial" links to plain Google Form (off-site, unbranded)
- "Register" links to Zen Planner (different external platform)
- Dual-CTA creates confusion — which button do I click?
- No sticky mobile CTA
- No click-to-call button
- No contact form anywhere
- **Fix:** Native embedded booking form, single primary CTA, sticky mobile CTA, contact form

### Content & Copy
- Homepage H1 is generic: "Welcome to Kids Soccer Academy – Wyckoff, NJ"
- Should be value-prop: "Bergen County's Premier Indoor Soccer Training for Ages 18 Months to High School"
- Copy is repetitive — "confidence, coordination, and discipline" recycled across sections
- Wall-of-text overwhelm on homepage
- No video content (despite YouTube/TikTok accounts linked in footer)
- No blog/content section for SEO
- Schedule page appears empty/broken

### Design & Technical
- Wix template feel is obvious
- Image sizing inconsistent
- Hero carousel shows rendering artifacts
- Heavy image carousel likely slows mobile load
- No embedded Google Map
- No schema markup beyond Wix defaults
- Heading hierarchy issues (jumps to H4)
- Hero images lack alt text

## Pitch Strategy

**Core narrative:** "You have gold and you're hiding it in a vault."

1. **Social proof gap:** 33 five-star reviews that no website visitor ever sees
2. **Conversion infrastructure:** Replace Google Forms with professional embedded booking
3. **Human element:** Zero coach bios on a business that asks parents to trust them with toddlers

**Meeting approach:** Build the site to ~85-90% completion speculatively. Show it at meeting. Leave a few placeholder spots (coach bios, video slots) to create engagement. Demo on mobile. Present $175/month retainer.

**What NOT to pitch yet:** PAL Calls, parent portal, gamification system, chat widget. These are all Phase 2+ upsells after trust is established.
